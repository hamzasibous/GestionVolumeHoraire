# PRD: Système de Gestion Volume Horaire

## Project Overview
A web-based system for the Computer Science Department to manage, track, and forecast teaching hours (CM, TD, TP). The system aims to ensure equitable distribution among faculty and provide clear statistical insights.

## Actors
- **Department Head / Administrator:** Full management of programs, faculty assignments, and forecasting.
- **Enseignant (Teacher):** Consultation of personal workload and assignments.

## Core Features
- **Program Management:** Create and manage 'filières', modules, and courses.
- **Resource Management:** Manage faculty and classrooms (locaux).
- **Hour Distribution:** Equitable allocation of CM, TD, and TP hours.
- **Statistics:** Global volume, departmental average, and type breakdown (TP/TD/CM).
- **Forecasting:** Simulation for the following academic year based on current trends.

## Technical Requirements
- Web-based administrative and consultation interfaces.
- Statistical calculation engine.
- Automated/Semi-automated distribution module.
